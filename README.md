# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

Comprehensive data analysis and visualization project showcasing sales trends, product performance, and customer insights from a multi-location coffee shop chain. This project demonstrates advanced Excel analytics, dashboard design, and business intelligence techniques.

## Dataset

**Data Coverage:** January 1, 2023 - June 27, 2023

**Dataset Size:** 150,000+ transactions

**Key Fields:**
- Transaction ID & Date/Time
- Store Location (Astoria, Hell's Kitchen, Lower Manhattan)
- Product Category (Coffee, Tea, Drinking Chocolate, Bakery, Branded Items)
- Product Type & Detail
- Transaction Quantity & Unit Price
- Total Sales Amount

## Key Insights & Metrics

### Sales Performance
- **Total Revenue:** $1,078,380
- **Average Transaction Value:** $7.50+
- **Best Performing Location:** Lower Manhattan ($4,624.51 total footfall)
- **Peak Sales Hours:** 7-9 AM (Morning rush)

### Product Analysis
- **Top Product:** Barista Espresso ($1,901.55 in sales)
- **Category Distribution:**
  - Coffee Products: 54.5% of sales
  - Tea Products: 22.3% of sales
  - Bakery Items: 15.2% of sales
  - Drinking Chocolate & Others: 8% of sales

### Store Locations
- **Astoria:** $4,609.01 revenue
- **Hell's Kitchen:** $5,959.71 revenue
- **Lower Manhattan:** $4,624.51 revenue

### Temporal Trends
- **Best Day:** Friday with consistent high sales
- **Hourly Pattern:** Peak orders during 7-9 AM and 12-1 PM
- **Seasonal:** Steady demand throughout the period

## Visualizations Included

1. **Quantity Ordered Based on Hours** - Line chart showing hourly trends
2. **Categories & Distribution Based on Sales** - Pie chart revealing product mix
3. **Size Distribution Based on Orders** - Breakdown of product sizes (Small, Regular, Large)
4. **Footfall & Sales Over Store Locations** - Comparative analysis across branches
5. **Orders on Weekdays** - Bar chart showing day-of-week patterns
6. **Top 5 Products Based on Sales** - Ranking of best-selling items
